Why Choose Axiom?

More than just a to-do list. A complete system for your life.

Deep Organization

Break down complex projects with hierarchical subtasks. Create up to 2 levels of depth to keep everything structured.

Smart Quick Notes

Capture ideas instantly with "Smart Drafts". Axiom saves your thoughts as drafts and reminds you to complete them later.

Premium Design

Experience fluid glassmorphism, modern gradients, and a stunning Dark Mode that is easy on the eyes.

Flexible Tags

Categorize with freedom. Use custom tags like #work or #home to filter and focus on what's actionable.

Punctual Reminders

Never miss a deadline. Set precise dates and times, and let Axiom handle the notifications.

Privacy First

Your data stays yours. Axiom is built with security and privacy as core pillars of the experience.
